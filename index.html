Expansion Framework

CBD

Central Business District

Core commercial zones. High visibility, established footfall, premium positioning.

PBD

Peripheral Business District

Emerging zones outside the center. Growing catchment, better unit economics.

TBD

Tertiary Business District

Outer commercial pockets. Local catchment, format-dependent viability.

Every trade area is evaluated using our DCB Framework: Demographics · Commercial · Brandscape

Stage Three:
Stock Matching & Execution

We identify available properties or source suitable stock within the agreed trade areas. Then we close the lease. Where relevant, we review underperforming locations to assess relocation or deal restructuring opportunities.

About Foottfall

We are a retail real estate leasing and advisory firm engaged in connecting brands and spaces through thoughtful location strategy. We work with hospitality-led and experience-driven brands to identify sites that offer the right mix of footfall and long-term potential. At the same time, we collaborate with landlords and developers to align their assets with brands that contribute meaningfully to value and engagement.

Our approach brings together trade area insight, market understanding, and execution, with the objective of creating outcomes that are well-matched, considered, and built for continuity.
