How We Work

Stage One:

Stage Two:
Expansion Framework

We build a location-led expansion strategy with a trade area opportunity matrix - showing you which CBDs, PBDs, or TBDs match your format, and why. We factor in demographics, commercial fabric, brandscape, and catchment glorifiers (nightlife clusters, malls).

CBD

Central Business District

Core commercial zones. High visibility, established footfall, premium positioning.

PBD

Peripheral Business District

Emerging zones outside the center. Growing catchment, better unit economics.

TBD

Tertiary Business District

Outer commercial pockets. Local catchment, format-dependent viability.

Every trade area is evaluated using our DCB framework: Demographics · Commercial Fabric · Brandscape

Stage Three:
Stock Matching
& Execution

We identify available properties or source suitable stock within the agreed trade areas. Then we close the lease. Where relevant, we review underperforming locations to assess relocation or deal restructuring opportunities.
